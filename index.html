Welcome Home, CETians

No matter where life has taken you, CET will always remain a place where friendships were forged, dreams took shape, and lifelong journeys began.

The College of Engineering Trivandrum Alumni Association (CETAA) serves as a vibrant platform that reconnects alumni with their classmates, faculty, and institution while fostering meaningful opportunities to contribute to the continued excellence of CET.

Whether you graduated decades ago or have recently stepped out of campus, you remain an integral part of the CET family.

About CETAA: A Legacy That Connects Generations

For decades, CETAA has united thousands of alumni across India and around the globe. Beyond reconnecting classmates, the association actively supports the growth of the institution through infrastructure development, student mentoring, scholarships, technical initiatives, career guidance, and community engagement.

Alumni Impact: The Strength of Our Community

Our alumni are making a difference in every corner of the world. From pioneering research laboratories and global technology companies to government institutions, entrepreneurial ventures, academia, healthcare, and public service, CETians continue to lead with knowledge, innovation, and purpose.

Through CETAA, this remarkable network comes together to support students, share expertise, mentor young professionals, and contribute to transformative projects that enrich our alma mater.
