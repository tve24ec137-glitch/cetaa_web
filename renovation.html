Diamond Jubilee Hall Renovation

Preserving Our Heritage. Creating Tomorrow's Landmark.

Project Overview

The Diamond Jubilee Hall Renovation Project aims to modernize one of CET's most significant campus facilities while preserving its historical and emotional significance.

The renovation focuses on creating a contemporary, comfortable, and technologically advanced venue capable of hosting academic conferences, international seminars, alumni gatherings, student events, cultural performances, and institutional celebrations. Our vision is simple—to create a facility that every CETian will proudly call their own.

Why This Renovation Matters

A Space That Shaped Generations
Every alumnus has a memory associated with the Diamond Jubilee Hall. It is where friendships deepened, achievements were celebrated, ideas were shared, and milestones were marked.

With changing expectations and growing institutional needs, significant upgrades have become essential. By investing in this project today, we are preserving a space that will continue to inspire future generations of engineers, innovators, entrepreneurs, and leaders.

Project Highlights

State-of-the-art upgrades for a world-class venue

Modern Air-Conditioning

Creating a comfortable environment suitable for conferences, seminars, examinations, and large gatherings year-round.

Upgraded Interiors

Improved seating arrangements, elegant finishes, modern lighting, and enhanced acoustics for a premium experience.

Smart Event Infrastructure

Contemporary audio-visual facilities designed to support hybrid events, presentations, and international conferences.

Ancillary Facilities

Improved utility spaces, green rooms, storage, support areas, and enhanced visitor amenities.

Accessible Design

Barrier-free access ensuring the hall is welcoming, safe, and fully inclusive for everyone.

Project Objectives

Transforming the Hall for the Next Generation

  • Install a modern centralized air-conditioning system for year-round comfort.
  • Upgrade electrical, lighting, and audio-visual infrastructure.
  • Modernize interiors with improved seating, flooring, acoustics, and aesthetics.
  • Develop functional ancillary spaces to support academic and institutional activities.
  • Improve accessibility for all users through inclusive infrastructure.
  • Enhance safety, sustainability, and long-term operational efficiency.
  • Create a venue that reflects the prestige and excellence of CET.
